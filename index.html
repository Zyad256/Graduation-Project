About this app

HanziHero is an immersive, gamified Chinese character (Hanzi) learning application. It blends retro RPG exploration, interactive dialogues, spaced repetition (SRS), stroke order drawing tutorials, and speaking exercises to transform how you study Chinese.
Explore Zenith City, talk to interactive NPCs, complete language quests, and unlock achievements. Build long-term memory with optimized spaced reviews, and master writing characters with real-time feedback drawing canvases.

Core Features

  • 🎮 Interactive RPG City (Zenith City): Built with the Flame engine. Walk around, enter buildings, and take quests in Chinese.
  • ✍️ Writing & Stroke Order: Draw directly on screen with animated guides and instant canvas evaluation.
  • 🗣️ Voice Recognition (STT) & Speech: Evaluate your pronunciation and speak directly with NPCs.
  • 🗃️ Spaced Repetition System (SRS): Optimize study intervals and track learning retention via Drift DB.
  • 📊 Gamification and Streaks: Level up, track XP, maintain a daily login streak, and unlock achievements.
  • ⚡ Shorebird OTA Hotfixes: Instantly receive patches and updates over-the-air!
